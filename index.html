Catch Me If You Can

2002

This is a fun tribute page to Catch Me If You Can, one of my favorite movies!

"The first time I watched this movie I was about 6 years old, too young to appreciate it. But, about 10 years later when I watched it again, I found a masterpiece."

Fun Facts!

  1. An incredible true story of Frank William Abagnale Jr., a man who cashed out about $2.5 million worth of bad checks and succesfully impersonated a Pilot and a doctor, ALL BEFORE REACHING ADULTHOOD!
  2. The movie is based on his book that came out in 1980.
  3. The real Frank Abagnale Jr. played a role in the movie
